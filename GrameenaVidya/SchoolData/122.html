A.U. High School is an Upper Primary with Secondary and Higher Secondary School in Visakhapatnam Urban. It was established in the year 1972 and the school management is Pvt. Aided. It's a Telugu Medium Co-educational school.


The school is situated close to the banks of Bay of Bengal Sea and is surrounded by mountains.The school is located at the Main City near Andhra University. It is situated amidst nice and pleasant environment with lot of breeze blowing through.
