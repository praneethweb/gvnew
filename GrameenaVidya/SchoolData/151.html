Appeal Letter to Donors

Dear Sir/Madam,

Sub: Sub: Adopt-a-School Program - Appeal for funding IT Infrastructure and Digital Learning

Despite the socio-economic disadvantage, our students have shown year on year improvements. We have achieved 94% pass during the academic year 2014-15. Our Alumni over the years have spread across Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, India and the world and have turned into responsible employees, family men and citizens. Our school is the heart beat of the neighborhood. It is the only affordable accessible school for families at the grassroots. Every student coming to us have big dreams and are excited to contribute their little bit for the betterment of all. Our teachers are dedicated to improving and motivating students to achieve more. Despite high odds of 1:48 teacher: student ratio and qualified teachers being underpaid due to budgetary constraints, day-in and day-out our teachers nurture our students with the right values and academics.

We strongly believe introducing technology into teaching and learning will propel our school to compete with the private schools and help our students score better and equip them with skills. We believe Grameena Vidya's and Donor's support and investment in our school will certainly create a conducive environment that will make everyone proud. I will be glad to provide additional information as needed.

Yours truly,

Principal

M V D M High School, Ward No 02 is a Upper Primary with Secondary and Higher Secondary School in Visakhapatnam Urban. It was established in the year 1957 and the school management is Local body. It's a Telugu Medium - Co-educational school.


The school is situated near King George Hospital, Which was oldest big Hospital in the city. Near Bay Bengal. We can call Hospital City also, there are so many Hospital are there. The school is 2 km away from Old Main City.
