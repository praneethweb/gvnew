Appeal Letter to Donors

Dear Sir/Madam,

Sub: Sub: Request to sponsor digital education in our school through Adopt-a-School Program

Zilla Parishad School Anadapuram is one of the integral institutions of the local community,through the years the school has been innovating means and techniques to retain students and provide a value based empowering education. The total free nature of the school and the added supply of uniforms, notebooks and stationery has made it very parent friendly. However, the teachers have limited teaching supplemental resources. The teacher - student ratio is very low. In order to impart quality academics and increase achievement levels of students as well as retention -AV room or Smart Classrooms will create a conducive learning environment that will provide life skills, employable skills, and reduce digital divide for these children who come from lowest social and economic strata of the population while preparing them to compete in the 21st century and to be able to compete with urban kids in terms of quality education.

Yours truly,
Mr.R.Sanyas Rao
Principal

The school is situated at Anadapuram colony where there is a famous flower market. It is a Road junction on the National Highway 5 between Visakhapatnam and Srikakulam.

Zilla Parisad High School was established in the year 1972 which was started with both Telugu and English Medium for classes 6th to 10th Standard. One of the students was selected for IIIT. The school Head Mater was awarded as “The Best Head Master” by Education Department & District Collector for his service.
