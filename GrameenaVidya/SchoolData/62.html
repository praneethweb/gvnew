GVMC MPS Allipuram is a Primary only School in Visakhapatnam Urban. It was established in the year 1953 and the school management is Local body. It's a Telugu Medium - Co-educational school.


GVMC MPS Allipuram runs in a government school building. The school has total 6 classrooms. This school is situated in the heart of the city and nearby famous vegetable and fruit market is available. This area is called Gandhi bomma center because Mr. Gandhi walked through that area. There is a library facility available in this school and the total number of books in library is about 40.
